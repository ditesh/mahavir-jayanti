This year's Mahavir Jayanti takes a new dimension as we have invited a professional singer Vijaybhai Doshi who has performed in many parts of the world with his melodious voice. Vijaybhai Doshi who also plays the musical keyboard will render beautiful, latest and lively stavans fit for this auspicious occassion of Mahavir Jayanti.

Together with Vijaybhai, we also will have Hiteshbhai Doshi in our midst who will complement Vijaybhai in his stavans. Hiteshbhai, a young and vibrant presenter, is able to reach the hearts of all present with his humour, simplicity of delivery in English/Gujarati for both young and old.

Please come together with your family and friends to make this auspicious occassion a memorable one for all and be blessed by
Bhagwan Shree Mahavir.
